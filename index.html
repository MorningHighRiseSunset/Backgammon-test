Instructions

Follow these steps to play Backgammon:

  1. Each player rolls one die, and the player with the higher number goes first. They use the numbers on both dice for their first move.
  2. Players take turns rolling two dice and moving their pieces according to the numbers rolled.
  3. Pieces move in a counterclockwise direction from the player's starting point.
  4. A piece can only move to an open point, which is one that is not occupied by two or more of the opponent's pieces.
  5. If a piece lands on a point occupied by a single opponent's piece, the opponent's piece is "hit" and placed on the bar.
  6. A player must move all their pieces off the bar before making any other moves.
  7. The objective is to move all your pieces into your home board and then bear them off the board.
  8. The first player to bear off all their pieces wins the game.
